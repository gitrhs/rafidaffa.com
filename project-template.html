Project Overview

PoernamaFest Store is the official merchandise platform for the PoernamaFest cultural festival. It offers a range of festival-themed items such as t-shirts, stickers, and accessories, all inspired by Indonesian heritage and global cultural diversity.
I worked as both full-stack developer and UI/UX designer to design and implement the platform. The store replaced the older Google Forms system with a seamless, user-friendly website that improved the shopping experience for fans.
